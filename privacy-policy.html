Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

The Short Version: Your data stays on your device. We don't sell your information. We don't track you. Period.

1. Introduction

CTRL ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When you use CTRL, you may choose to enter:

  • Your name (for personalization)
  • Bills and subscription information
  • Document details (names, expiry dates)
  • Tasks and to-do items
  • Habit tracking data
  • Calendar events

2.2 Data Storage

Local Storage: By default, all your data is stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable cloud sync.

Cloud Sync (Optional): If you create an account and enable sync, your data is stored securely in our cloud database (powered by Supabase) to allow access across devices.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your location
  • We do not access your contacts
  • We do not read your messages or emails
  • We do not track your browsing activity
  • We do not use advertising identifiers

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Provide the CTRL app functionality
  • Send you reminders and notifications (if enabled)
  • Sync your data across devices (if you enable cloud sync)
  • Improve the app based on aggregate, anonymous usage patterns

4. AI Assistant (Ada)

CTRL includes an AI assistant named Ada. When you interact with Ada:

  • Your messages are sent to our AI provider (Anthropic) to generate responses
  • Conversations are not stored or used for training
  • Ada has access to your app data (bills, tasks, etc.) to provide personalized guidance
  • You can use CTRL without ever using Ada

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

We may share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting (Supabase), AI services (Anthropic) — only as needed to provide app functionality
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmissions use HTTPS encryption
  • Cloud-stored data is encrypted at rest
  • We use secure authentication methods

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored in the app
  • Export: Download your data as JSON or CSV
  • Delete: Permanently delete all your data from Settings
  • Opt-out: Disable notifications, cloud sync, or AI features at any time

8. Children's Privacy

CTRL is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email (if you have an account).
